# Orbit Pulse

Orbit Pulse is a workflow adoption intelligence prototype built to address one core enterprise SaaS problem:

> Workflows get created, but employees do not consistently use them.

This project was developed as part of a Product Management case study focused on improving workflow adoption, operational visibility, and long-term customer retention inside enterprise workflow platforms.

---

## The Problem

The case study revealed a recurring adoption pattern:

- Most workflows were triggered only by the workflow builder
- Employee participation remained low after initial rollout
- Workflow completion rates dropped significantly over time
- Teams continued relying on email and informal communication instead of structured workflows

The issue was identified as a behaviour and trust problem rather than a simple awareness problem.

---

## The Solution — Orbit Pulse

Orbit Pulse adds an adoption reinforcement layer on top of existing workflows.

Instead of rebuilding the workflow system, Orbit Pulse helps workflow builders and Ops Managers:

- identify workflow bypass behaviour
- detect workflow drop-off patterns
- monitor adoption health
- re-engage inactive users
- improve workflow completion consistency

The product focuses on visibility, reinforcement, and lightweight intervention mechanisms.

---

## Features

### Admin Dashboard
- Workflow adoption tracking
- Workflow health monitoring
- Step-level drop-off analytics
- Active intervention visibility
- At-risk workflow identification
- Builder action recommendations

### Employee Experience
- First-run workflow onboarding
- Lightweight nudges and reminders
- Re-engagement prompts
- Simplified workflow participation

---

## Product Thinking

Orbit Pulse is based on a simple insight:

> Awareness may start adoption, but habit and trust determine continued usage.

Employees often return to familiar communication channels because they feel faster, easier, and more reliable than formal workflow systems.

Orbit Pulse creates a closed feedback loop between:
- workflow usage
- builder visibility
- intervention actions
- adoption improvement
